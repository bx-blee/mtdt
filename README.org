
#+title: MTDT: Mail Templating, Distribution and Tracking Emacs Package


MTDT is part of Blee. Blee (ByStar Libre-Halaal Emacs Environment) is the Usage Environment of
BISOS (ByStar Internet Services OS). BISOS is the foundation
of the "Libre-Halaal ByStar Digital Ecosystem." ByStar is rooted in the model of proper governance of
polyexistentials which rejects the Western Intelectual Propoerty Rights (IPR) regime.

MTDT augments the Emacs email environment (Gnus) by providing templates
for mailings and distributions.

Consider MTDT as a Libre-Halaal Convivial equivalent of Constant Contact for emacs.

Documentation of MTDT is in the form of a series of related panels. Primary MTDT Blee-Panel is
[[file:./panels/mtdt/_nodeBase_/fullUsagePanel-en.org]].
With some limitations, MTDT can be used an a stand alone Emacs package.
Relevant part of BISOS and Blee that interact with MTDT are:

- MARMEE -- Multi-Account Resident Mail Exchange Environment
- LCNT -- ByStar Libre Content production, publication
- Mailngs, Names, Dist facilities of BISOS
